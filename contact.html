If you have questions about building your gaming PC or need help with choosing parts, the X-Mega-Saw (XMS) team is here to help. Each team member specializes in a different hardware component, from motherboards to cooling and graphics. Reach out to us through the XMS site or your assigned team contact for support and advice.

Who to Contact

Reach out to the XMS team members based on your hardware questions:

  • Motherboard – Kobe: Compatibility, installation, and specs.
  • GPU – Raquan: Graphics card options and performance advice.
  • CPU – Jack: Choosing the right processor for your build.
  • RAM – George: Memory types, speed, and installation.
  • PSU – Jesse: Power supply sizing and safety.
  • Storage – Wiremu: SSD vs HDD and how much space you need.
  • Cooling – Tipene: Fan placement, airflow, and thermal paste.
  • RGB – Tupac: Custom lighting and visual flair.
  • Case – Arthur: Case sizing and cable management.
  • Monitor – Jamie: Resolution, refresh rate, and display tips.
  • Keyboard & Mouse – Matarangi: Gaming peripherals and responsiveness.

How to Reach Us

You can contact the XMS team through your classroom portal, school email, or by visiting us during build lab hours. We're happy to answer questions, review your part selections, or help you troubleshoot your system. Whether you're stuck on installation or just need a recommendation, we've got your back!
