Focus on Motherboards

As the foundation of the entire XMS system, the motherboard plays a critical role. Kobe led this research, choosing options that balanced performance and cost:

  • Why It Matters: The motherboard connects and powers all other parts.
  • Top Choices: Budget (Gigabyte B550 Gaming X V2), Mid-Tier (MSI MAG B850), and Top-Tier (Gigabyte X870).
  • Features Considered: CPU support, DDR4/DDR5 RAM, PCIe 4.0/5.0, and built-in Wi-Fi.

Our Research-Based Approach

All parts in the XMS build were chosen based on real-world pricing, trusted NZ sellers, and performance benchmarks from sites like PB Tech, PriceSpy, Amazon, and hardware review platforms.

  • Safe Buying: Focus on warranty, seller reviews, and compatibility checks.
  • Team Collaboration: Each member researched their part and contributed to the final decision-making.
  • Learning Outcomes: Understanding how to research, compare, install, and safely purchase PC components.
